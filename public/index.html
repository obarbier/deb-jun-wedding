Our Story

This is how it started, and now we know it will never end.

  • How We Met

    Her version

    I met my future husband through my little sister Samy. To tell you the truth I was never interested in meeting any of her friends but somehow I went along with her proposal. We chatted a bit over the phone and met for the first time on 12/29/2011 at a “Fritay Night” in FL. He was looking handsome or whatever (lol) but I was determined to have him as a friend rather than a lover. Since that night though, we had become inseparable. Inseparable as in spending days and nights talking to each other for long hours. Long hours as in 12 straight hours on skype without any interruption. A record for both of us. At this point, he had become my best friend. I love seeing his face, listening to his voice, hugging him, just being with him. He is a helpless romantic and the sweetest guy I have ever known. Yes, God changed my life for the better when I met the guy on December 29, 2011.
